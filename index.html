About Us

Raghav Sales Corporation Established in the early 1990s as a manufacturing unit to manufacture the spare part of Flour mill Industries, Food & grain milling industries, etc. We are setting a milestone in the terms of product quality & delivery commitments to the popular name in the Flour mill, Rice mill & Daal mill machinery industry. We are well-known manufacturers, Suppliers & Service Provider of Flour mill, Rice, & Dall mill spare parts in all over India. Our products are made by using high-quality raw material and are appreciated for their high durability, strength, performance, etc. At Raghav Sales Corporation through personal professionalism, we deliver the best value products to improve the quality of life of your machine. We work with exceptional people to create satisfying relationships through respect, trust and responsibility. We strengthen these relationships through continuous improvement and a commitment to excellence. Be assured that Raghav Sales Corporation is always here to serve you, meet your milling requirements and we would like the opportunity to prove to you that we are the right company for your milling-related needs. We welcome you to Raghav Sales Corporation and invite you to do business with people who will work to earn your business and trust, each and every day.
