About Us

Raghav Sales Corporation Established in the early 1990s as a manufacturing unit to manufacture the spare part of Flour mill Industries, Food & grain milling industries, etc. We are setting a milestone in the terms of product quality & delivery commitments to the popular name in the Flour mill, Rice mill & Daal mill machinery industry. We are well-known manufacturers, Suppliers & Service Provider of Flour mill, Rice, & Dall mill spare parts in all over India. Our products are made by using high-quality raw material and are appreciated for their high durability, strength, performance, etc. At Raghav Sales Corporation through personal professionalism, we deliver the best value products to improve the quality of life of your machine. We strengthen these relationships through continuous improvement and a commitment to excellence.
